GAMING

Gaming is a popular form of entertainment where people play video games on devices like PCs, consoles, or phones, offering a wide variety of genres from fast-paced shooters to immersive role-playing games. It brings people together through multiplayer modes and online communities, while also supporting a booming competitive scene called esports, where players compete professionally for prizes. Beyond playing, gaming has grown into a major industry with streaming and content creation on platforms like Twitch and YouTube, and the creative process behind making games combines art, storytelling, and technology. Whether casual or competitive, gaming offers something for everyone to enjoy.
